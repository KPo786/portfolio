Project Showcase

Carnival Cruise Line - carnival.com Revamp

Client: Carnival Cruise Line | Role: UI/UX Analyst

A transformation project to enhance the user experience on the Carnival.com website. My role involved reviewing design system components in Figma, performing design QA, and conducting user research, which led to improved design consistency and usability.

Outcome:

Improved design consistency, usability, and collaboration between design and development teams.

Liberty Corporate - Single Service Platform

Client: Liberty Corporate | Role: Product Analyst

This project aimed to enhance customer experience for members, employers, and financial advisors through a unified web app. I conducted requirements analysis, UX design, delivered mockups, and aligned stakeholders to improve usability.

Outcome:

Increased customer satisfaction, improved digital adoption, and achieved cost savings through negotiated platform deals.

Capitec Bank - Interstellar

Client: Capitec Bank | Role: Product Owner

I led the re-engineering of a legacy file transmission system for high-volume payment transactions. My responsibilities included analyzing workflows, redesigning the UI, managing the backlog, and leading sprints for implementation.

Outcome:

Streamlined payment operations, reduced licensing costs, and delivered a modernized, user-friendly system.

Capitec Bank - Rapid Payments (PayShap)

Client: Capitec Bank | Role: Product Analyst | Scrum Master

This proof-of-concept project was for South Africa’s new immediate payment channel under BankServAfrica. I represented Capitec at industry design sessions, documented requirements, and delivered mockups and integration documentation.

Outcome:

Demonstrated feasibility of real-time payments, supporting future implementation across the SA banking sector.
